About Our Platform

FarmConnect is designed to empower gardeners, farmers, and community organizations with the tools and knowledge needed for successful food cultivation. Whether you're growing food on a balcony, in a backyard, or as part of a community project, we're here to help.

School Gardening Projects

Educational programs that integrate gardening into school curricula, teaching children about nutrition, science, and environmental stewardship.

Benefits

Hands-on learning, improved nutrition awareness, and connection to nature

Impact

Students develop lifelong healthy habits and environmental consciousness

Implementation

Raised beds, container gardens, and curriculum integration

Durban Rooftop Gardening

Urban agriculture initiative utilizing underused rooftop spaces in Durban for food production and community building.

Benefits

Utilizes unused space, reduces urban heat island effect, local food production

Impact

Increased food security, community engagement, and green infrastructure

Implementation

Container gardening, hydroponics, and lightweight soil mixes

Home Food Gardening & Basic Health Programme

Integrated approach combining food gardening with basic health education for holistic community wellbeing.

Benefits

Improved nutrition, physical activity, mental health, and food sovereignty

Impact

Reduced healthcare costs, stronger communities, and sustainable food systems

Implementation

Backyard gardens, nutrition workshops, and health screenings

Our Community Gardening Projects

Network of community gardens that transform vacant lots into productive green spaces for neighborhood use.

Khayelitsha Vertical Gardens

Innovative vertical gardening solutions in Khayelitsha township, maximizing food production in limited spaces.

Benefits

Space efficiency, high yield in small areas, and adaptability to urban environments

Food sovereignty in dense settlements, economic opportunities, and skills development

Implementation

Vertical planters, trellis systems, and container gardens
